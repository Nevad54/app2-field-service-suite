# Field Service Suite - Enhancement Request

## Features to Add

### 1. Inventory/Parts Management
- Track parts and materials inventory
- Add, edit, delete parts
- Track quantity on hand
- Link parts to jobs

### 2. Equipment/Assets
- Track customer equipment
- Equipment history
- Maintenance schedules
- Link equipment to customers

### 3. Quotes/Estimates
- Create quotes for customers
- Convert quotes to jobs
- Quote status tracking
- Pricing line items

### 4. Recurring Jobs
- Schedule recurring appointments
- Define recurrence patterns
- Auto-generate jobs from templates
